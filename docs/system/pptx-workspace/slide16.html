WORKING WITH CLAUDE

Steer Claude in the moment — five intent modes

Slow down and reason deeply

think carefully think step by step think ultra hard

Before complex decisions, architecture, or debugging

Stop — don't fill gaps

do not assume do not guess ask if unsure

When Claude seems confident without reading the file

Verify before moving on

validate your work check your work do user end to end testing

After implementation, to catch self-introduced errors

Challenge the approach

take the contrarian view ask the sub agents

Stress-testing a plan before committing to a direction

Control scope and output

tell me succinctly only change what I asked preserve references

Preventing scope creep, verbosity, or silent reference changes

All phrases are situational — you combine them. "Think ultra hard, do not assume, validate your work" before a risky refactor. See Claude Code best practices: code.claude.com/docs
